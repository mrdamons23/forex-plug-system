Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our plans and pricing

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. If you downgrade, the new rate will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all our plans. If you're not completely satisfied with the Forex Plug System, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments (Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT). For annual plans, we also offer bank transfer options. Contact our sales team for more information.

How do I add more trading accounts to my plan?

Additional trading accounts can be added to the Starter and Professional plans for $49/month per account. The Enterprise plan includes unlimited accounts so no additional purchase is necessary.

Is there a long-term contract or commitment?

No, there is no long-term commitment required. Monthly plans can be canceled at any time. Annual plans offer a significant discount but can also be canceled anytime, though refunds for the unused portion are provided at a prorated rate minus a small administrative fee.
