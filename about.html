Our Story

Forex Plug System was founded in 2019 by a team of experienced forex traders and software engineers with a mission to develop intuitive, powerful, and accurate trading indicators that give traders the edge in volatile markets.

After years of trading with conventional indicators and experiencing the limitations and false signals they often provide, our founding team committed to creating a solution that would change how day traders approach the market.

Through extensive research, algorithm development, and real-world testing, we developed our signature indicator system that has since helped thousands of traders worldwide improve their trading performance.
